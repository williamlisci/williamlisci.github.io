Why Blog

Understanding of Blogs

People who like to write blogs will go through three stages.

In the first stage, they just came into contact with Blogs and felt it was very fresh. They tried to choose a free space to write.

In the second stage, they found that the free space had too many restrictions, so they bought domain names and space and built independent blogs.

In the third stage, they felt that the management of independent blogs was too troublesome. It was best to let others manage it while retaining control, and they were only responsible for writing articles.

Each of us generates more and more data on the Internet. This information is a trace of our existence on the Internet and deserves our serious attention. But they are scattered on various websites. Many times it is difficult for us to aggregate them together, and there is no way to freely control the information arrangement of each website, so we need a space that can be dominated by ourselves - blogs.

Through blogs, we can record the trajectory of our lives and growth. It is not as fragmented as Twitter, nor as relational as Facebook. It is a private space.
